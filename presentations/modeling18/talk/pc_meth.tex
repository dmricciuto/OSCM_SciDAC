\ben

\een

\bri
\item<1-> \underline{Projection} $\qquad \qquad z_k=\frac{\langle f(\vxi) \Psi_k(\vxi)\rangle}{||\Psi_k||^2}$\\

The integral $\langle f(\vxi) \Psi_k(\vxi)\rangle=\int f(\vxi) \Psi_k(\vxi) \pi_\vxi(\vxi) d\vxi$ is estimated by...
\bbi
